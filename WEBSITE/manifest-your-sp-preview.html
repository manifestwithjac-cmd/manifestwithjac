What Is EFT Tapping?

EFT is a mind-body tool that helps you clear emotional blocks, calm your nervous system, and rewire limiting beliefs stored deep in the subconscious.

You gently tap on specific acupressure points while speaking out loud the emotions or patterns you want to shift. Your body receives a signal: "I'm safe. I don't have to hold this anymore."

Before each session, check in: when I think about this person, how strong does it feel from 0–10? Even a shift from a 7 to a 4 shows your nervous system is releasing what it no longer needs to hold.

The 3-Round Arc

Round One — The Truth. We speak the raw, honest truth. The fear, the longing, the obsession. We give it a voice so the body can finally release it. You can't heal what you won't acknowledge.

Round Two — Allowance. The softening. The compassion. We say: "It makes sense that I feel this way." Your nervous system is told: you're not bad, you're not broken. This is often where the first wave of release happens — like a deep exhale.

Round Three — Reprogramming. Now that we've cleared space, we plant something new. Real, grounded beliefs your body can actually start to hold. Subconscious rewiring at the identity level.

After all three rounds: give yourself a hug, squeeze, deep breath. Sit with one hand on your heart. Ask: what does the woman who already has this person know that I'm practicing believing?
